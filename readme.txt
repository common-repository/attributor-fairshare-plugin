== Description ==

= FairShare Widget =

Turn all your hard work into additional cash by FairSharing your blog posts with others. The FairShare syndication widget makes it easy for your readers to re-post your articles on their blog or website.  Advertising revenue generated by your articles on their site is shared between you and them. The plugin inserts the FairShare widget on all your blog posts, so everybody will be able to syndicate your articles and earn more ad revenue for you.

= Here's how the FairShare widget works: =

* Sign up for FairShare at http://fairshare.attributor.com
* Install the plugin on all blogs you registered with FairShare
* The FairShare widget will be inserted on all your new blog posts
* Readers of your blog are able to re-post your articles on their web site or blog
* Ad revenue earned on pages with your content is shared between you and them
* FairShare tracks all re-posted articles, collects the ad revenue, and sends you a check
* FairShare takes care of tracking down and taking care of anybody who doesn't abide by the rules

It's that simple!

== Installation ==

1. Go to the WordPress admin console
2. Click on "Plugins" in the left navigation section
3. Click on the "Add New" in the Plugins section
4. Search for the term "fairshare"
5. Click on "Install" to install the plugin

Once the plugin is installed and activated, the FairShare widget will appear on all of your posts by default. If you want to remove the widget from any post, please follow the steps below:

1. Click on "Posts" in the left navigation section.
2. Click on the particular post from which you want to remove the widget.
3. By default the FairShare Widget will appear on all blog posts. You can change this with the setting on the Edit Post page.
4. You can change the position of the button on your blog pages by clicking on the FairShare tab in the left navigation and dragging the FairShare widget to your desired location.
